ShowAroo is built with your privacy in mind. We do not collect, sell, or share your personal data with third parties for advertising or commercial purposes. This policy explains exactly what happens with any information related to your use of the app.

01

Information We Collect

ShowAroo does not collect or store any personal information on our servers. The app operates primarily using publicly available content and metadata from a third-party content data service.

If you choose to sign in with Google, the following information is accessed from your Google account and stored locally on your device only:

  • Your display name
  • Your profile photo URL
  • Your email address (used for account identification only)

If you choose to sign in with TMDB, your TMDB session token is stored securely in your device's secure storage to maintain your session.

02

How We Use Information

Any information accessed is used solely to personalise your in-app experience. Specifically:

  • Displaying your name and profile photo on the Profile screen
  • Maintaining your login session so you don't need to sign in every time
  • Saving your watchlist and preferences locally on your device

We do not use your information for advertising, analytics, profiling, or any purpose beyond the core functionality described above.

03

Data Storage

All user preferences and watchlist data are stored exclusively on your device using on-device storage (Hive local database). This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to ShowAroo servers — because ShowAroo has no servers.

Authentication tokens (for TMDB sessions) are stored in your device's encrypted secure storage and are never transmitted to or stored by ShowAroo.

04

Third-Party Services

ShowAroo integrates with the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Google Sign-In — used for optional authentication. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Content metadata provider — provides movie and TV show information, posters, and metadata. No personal data is shared with this service.
  • Streaming sources — ShowAroo links to third-party streaming sources via an embedded web view. ShowAroo does not control, host, or take responsibility for content or privacy practices of these sources.
05

Data Security

We take the security of your data seriously. Authentication tokens are stored using your device's built-in secure encrypted storage (Android Keystore / iOS Secure Enclave). We do not transmit sensitive data over unencrypted connections.

Since all data remains on your device, the security of your data is also dependent on the security of your device. We recommend keeping your device updated and protected with a screen lock.

06

Children's Privacy

ShowAroo is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will take steps to remove that information.

07

Your Rights

Since ShowAroo does not store your personal data on any servers, the following actions are entirely within your control:

  • Delete your data — uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data from your device
  • Sign out — signing out clears your session and locally cached profile information
  • Revoke Google access — you can revoke ShowAroo's access to your Google account at any time via Google Account Permissions
08

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal, operational, or regulatory reasons. When we make changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of ShowAroo after changes have been posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
