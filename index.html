ShowAroo is built with your privacy in mind. We do not collect, sell, or share your personal data with third parties for advertising or commercial purposes.

01

Information We Collect

ShowAroo does not collect or store personal information on our servers. The app uses publicly available content and metadata from a third-party content service.

If you sign in with Google, the following is accessed from your account and stored locally on your device only:

  • Your display name
  • Your profile photo URL
  • Your email address (for account identification only)

If you sign in with TMDB, your session token is stored in your device's secure encrypted storage.

02

How We Use Information

Information is used solely to personalise your in-app experience:

  • Displaying your name and photo on the Profile screen
  • Maintaining your login session between app launches
  • Saving your watchlist and preferences locally on your device

We do not use your information for advertising, analytics, or profiling.

03

Data Storage

All preferences and watchlist data are stored exclusively on your device using local on-device storage. This data never leaves your device — ShowAroo has no servers that store user data.

Authentication tokens are stored in your device's encrypted secure storage and are never transmitted to or stored by ShowAroo.

04

Third-Party Services

ShowAroo integrates with the following third-party services:

  • Google Sign-In — optional authentication, subject to Google's Privacy Policy
  • Content metadata provider — provides movie/TV information and posters. No personal data is shared.
  • Streaming sources — linked via embedded web view. ShowAroo does not host or control these sources.
05

Data Security

Authentication tokens are stored using your device's built-in encrypted secure storage (Android Keystore / iOS Secure Enclave). We do not transmit sensitive data over unencrypted connections.

Since all data remains on your device, its security also depends on your device's security. We recommend keeping your device updated and protected with a screen lock.

06

Children's Privacy

ShowAroo is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe your child has provided information through the app, please contact us and we will take steps to remove it.

07

Your Rights

Since ShowAroo does not store your data on any servers, you have full control:

  • Delete your data — uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data
  • Sign out — clears your session and cached profile information
  • Revoke Google access — via Google Account Permissions
08

Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. When changes are made, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of ShowAroo after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

09

Contact Us

For questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, contact us through the ShowAroo app's support channel. We aim to respond to all privacy-related inquiries within 72 hours.

You can also reach us by opening ShowAroo → Profile → About for support contact details.
